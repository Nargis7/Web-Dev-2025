Gulab Jamun

Ingrediants

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • oil

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar Remove from heat, add cardamom, and set aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder.
  3. Divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them into round balls.
  4. Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn golden brown.
